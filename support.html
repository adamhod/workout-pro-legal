Reporting a bug

To help me fix your issue as fast as possible, please include the following when you write in:

  • Your device model (e.g. iPhone 16 Pro)
  • Your iOS version (Settings → General → About → Software Version)
  • The WorkoutPro version (visible on the App Store listing, or under Account → Menu)
  • A short description of what you were doing when the issue happened
  • The exact steps to reproduce it, if possible
  • A screenshot or screen recording, if the bug is visual

Common questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, tap the Account tab, tap the menu icon in the top-right, and choose Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, all workout history, and your local profile photo.

How do I sign out?

Account tab → menu icon → Sign Out.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap Forgot Password? and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link — it may take a few minutes to arrive.

Where does my workout data live?

Your account and workout data are stored in Supabase (a secure cloud database). Your profile photo is stored only on your device — it is never uploaded. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.

The suggested weight for a new exercise feels too heavy or too light — is that normal?

Starting loads are calculated to be a safe starting point for new gym goers. You can modify the load on any set — the program will adapt for subsequent sessions based on what you actually lifted.

What does "each" or "per dumbbell" mean on dumbbell exercises?

For dumbbell exercises, the main weight shown is the combined weight of both dumbbells. Beneath it, a smaller line shows the weight of each individual dumbbell.
